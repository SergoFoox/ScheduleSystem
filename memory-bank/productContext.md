# Product Context — ASMS V3

## Vision
ASMS V3 is a web application for automated creation and management of academic schedules in universities.

The system acts as an intelligent assistant for dispatchers, reducing manual work, minimizing errors, and improving decision-making.

---

## Target Users

### Dispatcher (Admin)
- Creates and edits schedules
- Controls teacher workload
- Manages substitutions
- Publishes final schedules

### Teacher
- Views personal schedule
- Receives updates and substitutions
- Has workload constraints

### Student
- Views actual schedule
- Receives updates in real time

---

## Core Goals

### 1. Automation
- Automatic schedule generation based on constraints
- Minimal manual intervention

### 2. Fast Substitutions
- Automatic selection of replacement teachers
- One-click schedule update

### 3. Workload Control
- Weekly hour limits
- Semester progress tracking
- Part-time constraints (limited working days)

---

## Business Priorities
1. Schedule correctness (no conflicts)
2. Fast generation
3. Easy editing
4. Flexible substitutions
5. Transparent analytics

---

## Problem Statement
- Manual scheduling is time-consuming
- High probability of human errors
- No fast substitution mechanism
- Lack of centralized workload control
